Mystical Creature

One horn but all the mystical might!

a mythical and legendary animal depicted as a beautiful horse with a single horn. A unicorn can come in a variety of species including but not limited to, The Shadow Unicorn, Light Unicorn, rainbow unicorn, or the legendary moon light ultracorn! The legendary moon light ultracorn or LMLU for short is the rarest and strongest of the unicorn species. The LMLU uses the star and moon power to grow 10 times the normal size and once it's eyes glow green there is no stoping the magestic creature

The Slippery Gator

Alligators can weigh over 450 kg (1000 lb). Alligators have a powerful bite but the muscles that open the jaw are relatively weak. An adult human could hold the jaws of an alligator shut with their bare hands. Alligators eat a range of different animals such as fish, birds, turtles and even deer. Today one will try to taste the rare meat of the Unicorn! but will the squirley gator be any match for the mystical beast?
